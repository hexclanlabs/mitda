About MITDA

About Mangalore IT Dealers Association (MITDA)

Mangalore IT Dealers Association (MITDA), is a 16 years old Registered Association which represents over 95+ Members engaged in the businesses of Information Technology which includes Hardware and Software Sales & Service, Distribution and Sub Distribution, IT Retail, IT System Integrators, IT Networking and Infrastructure Providers & Software Resellers.

With its Members standing united, MITDA, grows Stronger and Blooms year after year.


Founded in 2007, MITDA, today is one of the most successful IT Association in the country. We at MITDA regularly conduct Workshops, Seminars, Promote Social & Cultural programs for its members. MITDA also interacts with various OEMs, Distributors, Resellers, Authorized Service Providers and Government Agencies for any of the concerns or issues raised by the members.
